About iDeyPay

Founded in October 2016, iDeyPay is a digital currency wallet and platform that leverages on the blockchain technology to provide secure payment solutions. Bitcoin as a digital currency, is the world's most widely used alternative currency with a total market cap of over $100 billion. The bitcoin network is made up of thousands of computers run by individuals all over the world. Our aim is to provide trustworthy trading services for all, while continuously working to improve our scope to create a world that is financially borderless.
